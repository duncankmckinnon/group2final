Abandoned Property Dates

The database includes the eventual outcome for each abandoned property, and the date on which the outcome was completed. You can restrict the map and the plot of the outcomes to a specific date range using the 'Abandoned Property Dates' slider above.

School Rating

The database includes a performance rating for each school in the South Bend schoool district. You can restrict the map and the table to show only those schools within a specific rating range using the 'School Rating' slider above.

Business License Issue Date

This page can be used to evaluate the impact of abandoned property resolution on new business licenses. Adjust the date of abandoned property resolution or business license issue using the date sliders above. Isolate the data to a particular city council district with the drop down menu.
